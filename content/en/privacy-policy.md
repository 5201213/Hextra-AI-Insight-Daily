### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking tech to keep tabs on activity on our Service and store info. These tracking technologies include things like beacons, tags, and scripts, all used to collect and track info, and generally improve and analyze our Service. The tech we use could be:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file chilling on your device. You can tell your browser to ditch all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But hey, if you don't take Cookies, you might miss out on some parts of our Service. Unless you've set your browser to reject 'em, our Service might just use Cookies.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might have tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These awesome little files let the Company (for example) count users who have hit up those pages or opened an email, and for other cool website stats (like seeing how popular a section is and verifying system and server integrity). Pretty neat, right?

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you're offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the purposes set out below:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super vital for hooking you up with services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They help verify who you are and stop any funny business with user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you asked for just can't be delivered, and we only use these Cookies to give you those services. No kidding!

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to figure out if users have accepted the use of cookies on the Website. Simple as that!

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're cruising around the Website, like keeping your login deets or language prefs locked in. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience, so you don't have to punch in your preferences every single time you use the Website. Sweet, right?

For more info about the Cookies we use and your options regarding Cookies, hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
