### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep tabs on activity on our Service and store certain information. The tracking technologies we use include beacons, tags, and scripts. They help us collect and track info, and generally make our Service better and help us analyze how you use it. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file placed on your Device. You can set your browser to refuse all Cookies or to let you know when one is being sent. But hey, if you don't accept Cookies, you might not be able to use some parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject Cookies, our Service might just use 'em.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might contain small electronic files called web beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These awesome little files allow the Company to, for example, count users who have visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like checking out how popular a certain section is and verifying system and server integrity).

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super crucial to give you the services available through the Website and to let you use some of its cool features. They help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for just can't be provided, and we only use these Cookies to hook you up with those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies figure out if users have accepted the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you use the Website, like remembering your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you use the Website.

For more deets about the Cookies we use and your choices regarding them, hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
